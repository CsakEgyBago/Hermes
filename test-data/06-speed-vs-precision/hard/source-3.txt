The annual employee survey response rate hit an all-time high of eighty-five percent. A new wellness stipend was introduced this year. Building maintenance repainted the lobby over the weekend.

The wellness stipend can be used for anything from gym memberships to therapy copays, a deliberately broad scope that HR says was chosen after feedback from last year's survey specifically asked for more flexibility than a narrower fitness-only benefit would have offered. Uptake has been strong in the first few months, with a majority of eligible employees having submitted at least one reimbursement request. The lobby repaint was originally scheduled for later in the year but got moved up after a minor water damage issue required patching part of the wall anyway.
