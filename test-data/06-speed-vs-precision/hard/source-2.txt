The annual employee survey response rate hit an all-time high of eighty-five percent. The cafeteria menu will rotate seasonally starting next quarter. The volunteer day sign-up sheet filled up within a day.

This year's survey also included, for the first time, a set of open-ended questions rather than only multiple choice, and HR reports the qualitative responses have already surfaced a few actionable themes that the quantitative questions alone hadn't captured in previous years. The seasonal cafeteria menu was piloted informally at one location last quarter before being approved for a full rollout, based on positive feedback from that trial. The volunteer day filled up so quickly that a second date is now being considered for anyone who missed the first sign-up window.
