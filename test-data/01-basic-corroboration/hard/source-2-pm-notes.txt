Because of a critical security bug found during final testing, the launch slipped by one week. The beta tester group was expanded to catch similar bugs earlier next time. Stakeholders were notified via email on Monday. The updated launch date will be confirmed once the fix passes a final security audit. Support macros were updated to reflect the new timeline. A short FAQ was drafted for the sales team ahead of customer calls.

The PM notes record that most stakeholders reacted calmly to the Monday email, with only two follow-up questions arriving before end of day, both about whether the new date was firm or still tentative. The sales FAQ was drafted jointly with a support representative to make sure the language used on customer calls matched what support agents were already saying in tickets, avoiding any confusing mismatch between teams. Expanding the beta group is expected to roughly double the number of external testers by the next release cycle, and the PM has asked engineering for a rough estimate of how much additional triage time that will require. A short risk log was also started to track any other features that might need extra scrutiny before the rescheduled launch.
