Starting next month, parking prices downtown will rise twenty percent. Residents have until the end of the week to submit comments. An online form was set up to collect resident feedback.

So far the feedback has been mixed, with roughly as many comments supporting the change as opposing it according to a preliminary tally shared by the city clerk's office. Several commenters asked whether a reduced rate could be offered for residents with a downtown parking permit, a question the council has said it will address in a follow-up session. The comment period was originally going to be shorter but was extended by three days after a technical issue briefly took the online form offline.
