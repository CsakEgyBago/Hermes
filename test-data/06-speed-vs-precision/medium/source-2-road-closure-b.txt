Main Street will be closed for six months while the bridge undergoes repairs, the council confirmed Tuesday. A detour route was announced. Merchants along the closure zone will get temporary loading permits.

The detour adds roughly four minutes to the average commute according to the city's own traffic modeling, though officials acknowledge that estimate could rise once the closure is actually in effect and drivers adjust their habits. Temporary loading permits will let delivery trucks access side streets that are normally restricted during business hours, a concession the council added after hearing directly from affected merchants during an earlier planning session. A dedicated hotline has been set up for businesses to report any unexpected access issues once construction begins.
