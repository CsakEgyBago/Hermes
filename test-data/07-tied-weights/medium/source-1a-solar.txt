The rooftop solar panels were installed last spring. The installation crew finished a day ahead of schedule.

The panels cover roughly sixty percent of the available roof space, with the remaining area left open for future HVAC equipment access rather than being fully built out. Facilities chose a slightly more expensive panel model than originally budgeted after an energy consultant projected a meaningfully faster payback period based on the building's specific sun exposure throughout the day. The installation crew's early finish was attributed to unusually cooperative weather during the project window, which allowed them to work extended hours on two of the days without any weather-related delays.
