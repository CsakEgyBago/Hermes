Survey respondents most often credited remote work flexibility with boosting morale over the past year. Facilities upgraded the HVAC system in building two. The bike-to-work challenge saw record participation this spring.

The HVAC upgrade addressed a longstanding complaint about uneven temperatures on the upper floors of building two, an issue that had generated a steady trickle of facilities tickets for the better part of two years before finally being prioritized this cycle. Early feedback since the upgrade has been almost entirely positive, with several employees specifically mentioning the difference in an informal facilities survey sent out afterward. The bike-to-work challenge's record turnout is partly attributed to unusually mild spring weather, though organizers also credit a new mobile app that made logging rides noticeably easier than the spreadsheet-based system used in previous years.
