Feedback on the new expense reporting tool has been mostly positive so far. The new expense reporting tool rolled out to all departments this month. One department asked for an extra walkthrough session. The help article was updated with clearer screenshots. Adoption numbers are being tracked on an internal dashboard.

The department that requested an extra walkthrough works heavily with international travel expenses, which involve a handful of edge cases the original training material didn't cover in much depth. That extra session has since been recorded and added to the wiki for anyone else who runs into similar cases. The adoption dashboard currently shows just over eighty percent of eligible employees having logged into the new tool at least once, a number the product team considers a strong start for the first month.
