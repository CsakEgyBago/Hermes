The onboarding guide was rewritten last Friday. A printable quick-start card was added to the welcome packet.

The rewrite was prompted by feedback from several recent hires who found the old version confusing in places, particularly the section covering account setup, which referenced tools the company had since replaced. The new version was drafted collaboratively by a small working group that included someone from HR, someone from IT, and two employees who had joined within the past two months and could speak directly to what had been unclear. The printable quick-start card condenses the most commonly needed information onto a single page, meant to sit on a new hire's desk during their first week rather than requiring them to dig through the full guide for basic questions.
