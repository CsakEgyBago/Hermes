The new expense reporting tool rolled out to all departments this month. A follow-up survey will go out next week. The product team plans to review responses during the upcoming retrospective. A short incentive was offered to anyone who responded early. Results will be anonymized before being shared broadly.

The survey itself is fairly short, deliberately kept to under ten questions so that completion time stays low and response rates stay high. Early responders will be entered into a small raffle rather than receiving a guaranteed reward, a choice the product team made to keep the incentive budget modest while still encouraging quick turnaround. The retrospective where results get reviewed is open to anyone who wants to sit in, not just the product team, and a calendar invite with the video link has already gone out company-wide.
