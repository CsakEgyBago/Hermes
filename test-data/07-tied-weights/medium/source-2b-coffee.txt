A new coffee machine arrived in the break room this June. Staff feedback on the upgrade has been positive so far.

Several people have specifically mentioned appreciating the built-in grinder, which the old machine lacked entirely, meaning anyone who wanted freshly ground coffee previously had to bring in their own bag of pre-ground beans. The new machine also supports a wider range of drink options, including a couple of specialty espresso-based drinks that weren't possible before. Facilities has said they'll keep an eye on how well the machine holds up over the next few months before deciding whether to roll out the same model to the smaller break room on the third floor.
