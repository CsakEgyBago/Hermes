Downtown parking rates will increase by twenty percent starting next month. The city council approved the change during Tuesday's meeting. A revised fee schedule will be posted on the city website by Friday.

The vote passed by a narrow five-to-four margin after nearly two hours of public comment, with several council members expressing reluctance despite ultimately voting in favor given the city's ongoing budget shortfall. The additional revenue is earmarked specifically for a backlog of pothole repairs that residents have been requesting for over a year. City staff estimate the new rate structure will generate roughly eight hundred thousand dollars annually once fully in effect.
