Bridge repairs will keep Main Street closed for roughly six months, according to city officials. Construction crews start next Monday. The project is expected to finish two weeks ahead of the original estimate.

City officials credit the accelerated timeline to a contractor who submitted an unusually aggressive bid promising faster completion in exchange for a modest bonus if they hit certain milestones early. The construction crew will work extended daytime hours but has committed to staying within standard noise ordinance limits in the evenings, following complaints about a previous project elsewhere in the city. A live progress tracker will be posted on the city's public works page once work begins, updated weekly throughout the closure.
