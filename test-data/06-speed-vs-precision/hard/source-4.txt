Employees pointed to the flexibility of working remotely as the biggest driver behind higher morale scores this year. The cafeteria menu will rotate seasonally starting next quarter. A charity bake sale raised money for the local shelter.

The remote flexibility in question isn't a fully remote policy but rather a hybrid arrangement allowing up to three days a week from home, which the survey data suggests hits a sweet spot for a majority of respondents compared to either fully in-office or fully remote setups tried at different points over the past couple of years. The bake sale exceeded its fundraising goal by a comfortable margin, with several departments running informal internal competitions to see who could bring in the most donated baked goods. Organizers are already planning a slightly larger version of the event for the spring.
