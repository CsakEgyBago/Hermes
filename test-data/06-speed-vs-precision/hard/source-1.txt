The annual employee survey response rate hit an all-time high of eighty-five percent. Remote work flexibility was cited as the top reason for improved morale. IT completed the laptop refresh program. A new badge reader was installed at the main entrance.

HR credits the record response rate partly to a redesigned survey that took roughly half the time to complete compared to last year's version, and partly to a raffle incentive offered to anyone who finished within the first week. The laptop refresh program replaced devices for just over four hundred employees company-wide, prioritized by device age rather than department. The new badge reader supports contactless entry via phone as well as the traditional physical badge, a change facilities has wanted to make for over a year.
