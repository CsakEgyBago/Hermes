The company relocated its headquarters to a new building downtown. The lobby now includes a dedicated visitor check-in desk.

The new building offers roughly twenty percent more square footage than the previous headquarters, which leadership says was a major factor in the decision given how much the company has grown over the past two years. The move was planned around a single long weekend to minimize disruption, with IT staging most of the network and server setup in the new location days ahead of time so that employees could show up Monday morning and get to work with minimal interruption. The visitor check-in desk is staffed during core business hours and includes a small waiting area with charging outlets, a detail several early visitors have specifically complimented.
