Local business owners are concerned about the new twenty percent downtown parking rate hike. Some worry it will reduce weekend foot traffic. A survey of nearby shop owners found mixed opinions overall.

A handful of business owners have floated the idea of validated parking as a way to soften the impact on customers, similar to programs used in a few neighboring cities. The local merchants association plans to raise the idea formally at the upcoming public hearing rather than waiting for the city to propose something on its own. Not every business owner surveyed was opposed, however; a few noted that better-funded pothole repair could actually make the downtown shopping district more appealing overall, even with pricier parking.
