The auth service migration to the new identity provider is now complete. Finance closed out the migration budget line this week. The migration budget came in slightly under the original estimate. A closing reconciliation report will be shared with stakeholders.

Finance tracked the migration project separately from the rest of engineering's quarterly budget specifically because of how large and cross-functional it was, which made it easier to report a clean final number rather than having the cost blended into general engineering spend. The modest underspend is mostly attributed to the vendor contract coming in slightly cheaper than the initial quote after a round of negotiation, rather than any change in project scope. The closing report will also include a rough estimate of ongoing operational cost under the new identity provider, to help set expectations for next year's budget cycle.
