Headquarters relocated to a new downtown building this year. Employees completed the move over a single weekend.

Moving crews handled desks and shared equipment, while employees were asked to pack and personally transport their own personal items, a split that most people found reasonable given how quickly the overall timeline came together. A dedicated help line was staffed throughout the weekend for anyone who ran into an issue locating their equipment or getting set up at their new desk. By Monday morning, the vast majority of employees reported being fully operational, with only a handful of minor issues logged, mostly related to network drive access that IT resolved within the first few hours.
