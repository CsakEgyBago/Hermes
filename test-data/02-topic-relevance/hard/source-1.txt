The new expense reporting tool rolled out to all departments this month. Training sessions are scheduled throughout the week. The finance team held an optional Q&A session on Wednesday afternoon. A printable quick-reference guide was added to the internal wiki. Managers were asked to remind their teams about the rollout during standups.

Attendance at the Q&A session was higher than finance expected, with nearly forty people joining despite it being scheduled during a typically busy part of the week. Most questions centered on how to handle expense reports that were already in progress when the new tool went live, and finance clarified that anything submitted before the cutover date should still be processed under the old system. The quick-reference guide has already been updated once based on feedback from the Q&A, adding a section specifically about mileage reimbursement that wasn't covered in the original version.
