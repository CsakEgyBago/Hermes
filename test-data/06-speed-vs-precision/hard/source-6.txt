According to the survey, greater flexibility to work remotely was the leading factor behind improved employee morale. Parking permits will be renewed automatically this year. A shuttle service pilot begins this fall.

Automatic renewal for parking permits replaces a manual application process that had generated a fair number of complaints in past years about paperwork getting lost or renewals being processed late. The shuttle pilot will run between the main office and the satellite parking lot roughly a mile away, aimed at employees who'd rather not pay for closer parking now that rates have gone up. If the pilot proves popular, facilities has said they'll consider adding a second route to a nearby transit station as a further extension.
