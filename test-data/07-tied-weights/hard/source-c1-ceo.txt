A new CEO was appointed following a unanimous board vote. The transition period will run through the end of the quarter. An internal memo thanked the departing leader for eleven years of service.

The incoming CEO previously served as the company's chief operating officer for the past four years, and the board's memo emphasized continuity as a major factor in the decision, noting that a significant amount of institutional knowledge was preserved by promoting from within rather than conducting an external search. The transition plan includes a series of joint appearances by the outgoing and incoming CEOs at town halls across every major office, intended to reassure employees and give them a chance to ask questions directly. Industry analysts covering the announcement generally described the move as low-risk given the incoming CEO's long tenure and deep familiarity with the company's existing strategy.
