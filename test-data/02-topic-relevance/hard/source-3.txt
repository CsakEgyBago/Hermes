The new expense reporting tool rolled out to all departments this month. Several clients were reimbursed for the shipping delays. The logistics partner apologized directly to affected accounts. A revised delivery estimate was posted on the status page. Warehouse staff traced the cause to a labeling error.

The labeling error affected a single batch that was misrouted to the wrong regional distribution center, adding several days to delivery times for orders in that batch. The logistics partner's apology included a small credit for future shipping on top of the reimbursement already issued, which several affected clients acknowledged positively in follow-up correspondence. Warehouse staff have since added an extra manual check at the labeling station specifically for batches heading to that region, since the automated system that produced the original error hasn't yet been fully diagnosed.
