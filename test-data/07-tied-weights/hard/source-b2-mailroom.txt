Package delivery in the mailroom moved to twice a day this month. A sorting shelf was added to speed up the process.

The new sorting shelf organizes packages by floor rather than by arrival order, which the mailroom staff say has cut the time it takes to prep each delivery run by a noticeable margin. The change to twice-daily delivery was piloted informally for two weeks before being made permanent, giving the team a chance to work out a few kinks in the new sorting process before committing to it long term. Feedback collected from a handful of frequently-shipped-to departments during the pilot was uniformly positive, which helped make the case for making the change permanent.
