# Quarterly Report

Revenue increased this quarter, driven mainly by stronger performance in the enterprise segment and a modest improvement in renewal rates across the existing customer base. The sales team closed several multi-year contracts that had been in the pipeline since the previous quarter, which contributed meaningfully to the headline number. Customer success also reported a noticeable drop in churn among accounts that adopted the new onboarding flow introduced earlier this year.

# Next Steps

Hire two more support engineers to keep pace with the growing ticket volume, and begin evaluating a second on-call rotation for the busier holiday season. Leadership also wants a proposal for expanding the self-service help center, since a growing share of tickets could plausibly be resolved without agent involvement if the right documentation existed. A follow-up planning session has been tentatively scheduled for early next month to prioritize these initiatives against the rest of the roadmap.
