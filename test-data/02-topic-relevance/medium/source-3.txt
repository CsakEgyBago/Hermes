The company picnic has been moved to the rooftop terrace. Building security badges will be reissued next month. The mail room will be relocated to the ground level. A bike rack was installed near the loading dock. The recycling program was expanded to include electronics.

The badge reissuance is being done in batches by floor to avoid a single-day rush at the security desk, with each floor getting a scheduled window over the course of the month. The mail room relocation is meant to make deliveries easier for the loading dock staff, who previously had to carry packages up two flights of stairs. Early feedback on the new electronics recycling bins has been positive, with several people mentioning they'd been holding onto old cables and chargers for exactly this kind of program.
